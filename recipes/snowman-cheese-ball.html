Snowman Cheese Ball

Description

Are you feeling lonely this Christmas? If so, then I have a perfect solution for you: make yourself an adorable cheese snowman friend! He will never abandon you. Well... at least not until he rots away... or you eat him alive... you monster

Quick Facts

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Chill Time: 4 hrs
  • Total Time: 4 hrs 15 mins
  • Servings: 24

Ingredients

  • 2 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (8-ounce) package shredded pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon salt-free Cajun seasoning, such as T-Donâ€™s Cajun Goods No Salt All Purpose Blend
  • 1 (1-ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
  • 10 whole peppercorns
  • 1 baby carrot
  • 1 jalapeno pepper with stem
  • crackers for serving

Directions

  1. Mix cream cheese, pepper Jack cheese, Cajun seasoning, and ranch dressing mix together in a large bowl; divide mixture to form one small cheese ball for the head and one larger cheese ball for the body. Wrap and chill in the refrigerator for 4 hours or up to overnight before serving.
  2. Place larger cheese ball on a serving plate for body. Place smaller cheese ball on top for head. Slice the tip off baby carrot; press into cheese ball for nose. Use whole peppercorns for mouth, eyes, and buttons. Cut the top, with stem, from a jalapeno, and place on top for a hat. Serve with crackers.
